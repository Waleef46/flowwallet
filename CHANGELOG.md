# Changelog

All notable changes to FlowWallet. Newest first.

## v1.1.0 — July 2026
- **Custom categories** — add / edit / delete your own categories (name, emoji, colour, type) from Settings. Ships with new defaults: Loans & EMI, Rent, Investments.
- **Auto-updating app** — service worker is now network-first, so updates apply automatically on reopen (no hard refresh needed) while still working offline.
- **In-app changelog & version label** in Settings.

## v1.0.0 — July 2026
- Dashboard with total balance, cash-flow chart, budget rings, and account balances.
- **Accounts & transfers** — Cash / Bank / Card with transfers between them.
- **Recurring transactions** — auto-post salary, rent, subscriptions, etc.
- **Transactions** with search, date-range filters, and CSV export.
- **Budgets** with monthly picker and circular progress.
- **Analytics** — spending by category, monthly trend, and insight cards.
- **Savings goals** with contributions.
- **Reports** — printable / save-as-PDF monthly statement.
- **Local login + profile** (name, password, stats).
- Multi-currency (INR, USD, MVR, EUR, GBP, AED), blue theme, installable PWA with offline support.
